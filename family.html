Family Is Everything
In this world the people who are always by my side are my family members. They are the ones who always inspire me to stay strong and the source of my happiness. They are also the reason for me to stay strong to live life. Ever since I was born into this world, I have seen them as I grew up. They are the ones who take care of and educate me until now. They are my father, mother and brothers. How lucky I was to be born into this small family. I really love them.



FATHER


This is my father. My father's name is Faizon bin Norhan. He was born on 27 October 1952 in Lambor Kanan, Perak. He is very fierce and firm towards his children but he loves his children very much. He is the one who educates and takes care of us. He is the head of our family. He was a headmaster and retired in 2011. My father passed away on October 28, 2013 at the age of 60 due to organ shock. After he died, we lost our hero and we miss him very much.





MOTHER


This is my mother. My mother's name is Noreshah binti Saaiddin. She was born on 17 December 1962 in Panchor, Perak. She is now 58 years old. She works as an Administrative Officer, Budget Division in the Economic Planning Unit, Prime Minister's Department. She is very gentle and loving and always follows the wishes of the children. She has been taking care of our family since my father died. Se is the mother and She is also our father.





FIRST BROTHER


This is my first brother. My brother's name is Fazlan Norsiwan bin Faizon. He was born on 21 July 1981 in Lambor Kanan, Perak. He is now 39 years old. He went to school until Form 5 only. He is self-employed. He now lives in Tapah to work. He is a fierce but loving person.





SECOND BROTHER


This is my second brother. My brother's name is Fitri Norfarhan bin Faizon. He was born on June 20, 1986 in Lambor Kanan, Perak. He is now 34 years old. He worked as a H11 Civil Assistant at the Manjung Municipal Council. He was married on October 29, 2020. He is a person who cares about our family. Since my father passed away, he has taken on the responsibility of being the head of the family. He sacrificed a lot for the family and always met our demands. He took care of our house in Bota, Perak while now my mother and I live in Putrajaya.





THIRD BROTHER


This is my third brother. My brother's name is Fareez Norfarhan bin Faizon. He was born on July 21, 1987 in Lambor Kanan, Perak. He is now 33 years old. He did various jobs such as repairing motors and cutting trees. He and I have an age difference of 13 years. He is the most diligent in doing housework among the four of us siblings.
